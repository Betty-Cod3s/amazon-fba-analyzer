Business Report

Upload your "Detail Page Sales and Traffic" or "by Child Item" report from Amazon Seller Central

How to download:
  • Click the help icon to open Business Reports
  • Go to "Detail Page Sales and Traffic" section
  • Select either:
    • "Detail Page Sales and Traffic" report, or
    • "Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child Item" report
  • Choose your date range (last 30-90 days recommended)
  • Download the report and upload it here

Inventory Report

Upload your "All Listings Report" from the Inventory section of Seller Central

How to download:
  • Click the help icon to open Seller Central Reports
  • Select report type: "All Listings Report (Custom)"
  • Click "Customize the columns for this report"
  • Make sure these attributes are selected:
    • item-name
    • price
    • fulfillment-channel
  • Click "Request Download" and wait for the report
  • Download the completed report and upload it here
